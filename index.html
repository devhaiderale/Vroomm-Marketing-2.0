Frequently Asked Questions

How does the "No-Burn Guarantee" work?

We use secondary domains (e.g., get-company.com) that redirect to your main site. This ensures your primary domain reputation is never at risk. If a domain is burned, we replace it instantly at our cost.

What counts as a "Qualified Lead"?

A qualified lead is a prospect matching your ICP (Industry, Size, Title) who has expressed positive interest in a meeting or demo. OOO replies or "not interested" do not count.

Do I need to provide the leads list?

No. We source verified leads using our own data partners (Apollo, ZoomInfo, etc.). However, if you have a specific ABM list, we can target those accounts specifically.
